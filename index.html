The Honest Truth About Wax Boxes

Wax-coated cardboard produce boxes were designed to solve one problem: cardboard falls apart when wet. The wax coating gives cardboard some moisture resistance for a single trip through the cold chain.

But wax boxes have a major problem — they're single-use, and the wax coating makes them nearly impossible to recycle. Most wax cardboard ends up in landfill. For high-volume operations shipping thousands of units per week, that's a significant cost and an environmental liability.

PP corrugated boxes from our top-rated manufacturer are 100% waterproof — not just moisture-resistant. They're reusable for 100+ cycles, pressure washable, food-grade, and 100% recyclable at end of life. The economics are overwhelming once you're moving at scale.
